Ginger cookies

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter - softened (but not melted)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar - divided
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup blackstrap molasses - or dark molasses
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • 1½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 2¼ cups gluten free all purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180 °C
  2. In a large bowl, cream the butter, ½ cup of the cane sugar and the brown sugar with an electric mixer.
  3. Add the molasses and eggs and continue mixing until it’s well combined. Then add the ginger, cinnamon, cloves, sea salt and baking soda and mix well.
  4. Last, add the Bob’s Red Mill gluten free flour and mix until well combined. The mixture will be slightly wet.
  5. Roll the gingersnap cookie dough into 1-inch balls, then roll each ball in the remaining ½ cup of cane sugar. Lay the ball on a parchment paper lined baking sheet. Repeat until you’ve used all the dough.
  6. Bake for 13-15 minutes or until the cookies are crackled and set. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for about 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool to room temperature.

Enjoy!
