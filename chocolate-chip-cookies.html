Chocolate-chip-cookies

Ingredients

  • 165g butter
  • 300g granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp of grounded coffee
  • 1 tbsp vanilla essence
  • 2 eggs
  • 350g of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • a pinch of salt
  • And of course 175g of chopped chocolate

Instructions

  1. In a mixer beat the butter, sugar and the coffee together until creamy
  2. Add in the eggs one at a time as well as the vanilla essence and whisk
  3. In a separate bowl whish the flour, baking soda as well as the salt
  4. Combine the dry ingredients the wet and then incorporate the chocolate
  5. Make balls out of the dough and place onto baking sheet then bake at 180Â°C for 10 minutes

Enjoy!
